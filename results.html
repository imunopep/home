Tyr369-377 kinetic analysis of the oxidation potential, oxidative derivatives characterization and semi-preparative method for their purification
Using hydrogen peroxide an oxidation kinetic profile was obtined in vitro for the epitope and the oxidative derivatives were separated by liquid chromatography coupled with UV-VIS detection. Each derivative was confirmed using high-resolution mass spectrometry and by tandem mass spectrometry. The separation method was up-scaled for the purification of increasing amounts for subsequent use.
